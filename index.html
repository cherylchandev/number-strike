How to Play

  1. An equation will appear at the top of the screen. Players must solve for the ? within 20 seconds.
  2. Throw the correct number of knives, then click the ✔ Submit button at the bottom-right corner. The center of the oak target will display the number of knives thrown.
  3. Players can click the ↺ Retry button at the bottom-left corner if they throw the wrong number of knives.
  4. Controls:
    • Mobile: Tap the screen.
    • PC: Use the mouse click; press Space to throw knives, press R to retry, and press Enter to submit an answer.
  5. There are ❤ ❤ ❤ at the top-right corner. One heart will be deducted if players:
    • Submit a wrong answer.
    • Fail to submit an answer before the timer ends.
    • Throw knives that collide with each other.
    • Throw knives into an empty space on the oak target.
  6. Score calculation:
    • 10 points: Submit the correct answer within 20 seconds, or hit the bonus 🪙 coin when it appears outside the oak target.
    • 5 points: Retry and then submit the correct answer within 20 seconds.
